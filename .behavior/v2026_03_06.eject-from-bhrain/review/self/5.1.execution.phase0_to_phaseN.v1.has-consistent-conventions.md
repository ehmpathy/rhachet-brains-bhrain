### 6. slug format

**extant pattern** (from rhachet):
- atom slugs: `claude/sonnet`, `openai/gpt-4o`, `xai/grok`
- repl slugs: `claude/code`, `openai/assistant`

**our addition**:
- composite slug: `bhrain/arch1@claude/sonnet`
- repl part: `bhrain/arch1`
- atom part: `claude/sonnet`

**question**: does composite slug diverge from convention?

**analysis**:
- composite slug is NEW to rhachet ecosystem
- the `@` separator is intuitive: `$repl@$atom`
- it follows the pattern `{repo}/{capability}@{provider}/{model}`
- rhachet does not have composite slugs because other repls dont need them

**verdict**: ✅ new convention, but intentional and well-documented
